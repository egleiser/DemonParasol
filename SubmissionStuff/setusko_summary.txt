Setsuko (Adventure, Puzzle)

Setsuko is an isometric adventure game with puzzle and action elements . As Setsuko, the player uses a living parasol with a hookshot tongue to
help save Setsuko's mother. The game features a defensive combat system as well as a moving narrative expressed in voice-over, dialogue, and the gameplay itself.

Eric Gleiser - Tech Lead, Modeler, Sound Design
